About Instagrumble


*sarcastic tone* Instagrumble is a social network for pugs. Sometimes as a pug you just want to brag about your face flabs or what bone you had for dinner. Instagrumble aims to be a place for pugs to connect and meet with other similar canine friends.

Really though,

My name is Connor, and I'm a developer. Recently the alpha for bootstrap v4.0 was released and I couldn't wait to test it out. I like pugs and yeah. This site pulls data from Reddit via /r/pugs and puts it in a instagram inspired format. Clicking a username will bring you to the original submission comments, likes = upvotes, and posts are ordered newest-oldest.
